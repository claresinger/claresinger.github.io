Outreach/Leadership

Science outreach

I feel strongly that spreading awareness about science in general and climate science in particular is in our mandate as socially responsible citizens and scientists. And I love teaching kids about science because they are so excitable and naturally curious about our world. I had the pleasure of participating in Caltech's Science for March community outreach event in 2019.

Women in GPS

I am the vice president of the Women in Geophysical and Planetary Sciences group at Caltech (WinG for short!). We organize coffee chats with visiting professors, a monthly journal club to discuss social science research about gender and minority representation in the sciences, and social events for students in the GPS division at Caltech.

Caltech Hillel

I am the co-founder and treasurer of the the Caltech Hillel. As a group we work to build a community on campus and in Pasadena for Jewish students and allies.

Title IX Graduate Student Council

I have had the privilege of participating in the Giving Voice project and serving on the Title IX Graduate Student Council since 2019.
